What is ESG?
Environmental
Environmental scores portray how an organization considers the natural environment. Important factors include:
  • Climate change
  • Deforestation
  • Waste management and polution
  • Natural resource depletion
  • Greenhouse gas emissions
Best Companies
Social
Social scores address how the organization treats people and concentrates on:
  • Diversity, equity, and inclusion
  • Human rights, including child labor and slavery
  • Health and safety
  • Labor standards
  • Community engagement
Best Companies
Governance
Governance scores examine how an organization polices itself, and how it is governed and focuses on:
  • Company leadership
  • Corruption and bribery
  • Donations and political lobying
  • Executive remunerations
  • Board diversity and structure
